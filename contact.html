Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for affordable housing?

You can apply through the Monroe Housing Authority or directly with properties listed on our website. Each property may have different application requirements and income restrictions.

What documents do I need to apply?

Typically, you'll need identification, proof of income, Social Security cards for all household members, and references. Some programs may require additional documentation.

How long is the waiting list for Section 8?

Waiting list times vary depending on availability and demand. Currently, the average wait time is 12-18 months, but this can change. Check our resources page for current status.

What if I need immediate housing assistance?

For immediate needs, contact our emergency hotline at (318) 512-6373. We can connect you with emergency shelter options and rapid rehousing programs in the Monroe area.
