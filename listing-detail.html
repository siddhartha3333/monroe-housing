Description

Parkview Apartments offers affordable 1 and 2 bedroom apartments in the heart of downtown Monroe. Our well-maintained community features spacious floor plans with modern amenities. Water and trash service are included in your rent, making budgeting easier. The property is conveniently located near public transportation, shopping centers, and dining options.
