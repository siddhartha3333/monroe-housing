How to Apply for Housing Assistance

  1. Determine which program is right for you based on your income and needs.
  2. Gather required documentation (ID, proof of income, Social Security cards, etc.).
  3. Complete the application form for the specific program.
  4. Submit your application to the appropriate agency.
  5. Attend any required interviews or briefings.
  6. Wait for eligibility determination (this may take several weeks).

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program

The Housing Choice Voucher Program (Section 8) is a federal program that assists very low-income families, the elderly, and the disabled to afford decent, safe, and sanitary housing in the private market.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Income must be below 50% of area median income
  • U.S. citizenship or eligible immigration status
  • Satisfactory criminal background check
  • Good rental history

Application Process:

  1. Apply when waitlist is open
  2. Complete eligibility interview
  3. Receive voucher if eligible
  4. Find housing that accepts vouchers
  5. Pass housing quality inspection
